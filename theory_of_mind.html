Theory of mind AI

Definition

Theory of mind is the ability to attribute mental states — beliefs, intents, desires, emotions, knowledge, etc. — to oneself and to others. Theory of mind is necessary to understand that others have beliefs, desires, intentions, and perspectives that are different from one's own.

Uses of Theory of mind AI

  • It is more on the books and science fiction movies.
  • Here we talk about artificially intelligent and advanced systems that can perceive the concept of emotions and people and change their behaviour accordingly
  • We can recognize our face and eye movements and modify their acts accordingly.
